What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into the environment, causing adverse effects. It can have various forms and sources.

Types of Pollution

  • Air Pollution
  • Water Pollution
  • Soil Pollution
  • Noise Pollution
  • Light Pollution
Effects of Air Pollution


Air pollution is compossed of air particles that fall back to earth cuasing serious harm, to humans, plants, and even buildings. People experience a very wide range of health effects from air pollutants, with short-term effects being:
* Pneumonia
* Bronchitis
* Nausea

Long term effects being:
* Heart Disease
* Lung Cancer
* Emphysema

The environment is not safe from the pollution, entire ecosystems are suffering and facing extinction, contaminating water, soil, killing crops and reducing their yield drastically.
