What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into the environment, causing adverse effects. It can have various forms and sources.

Types of Pollution

  • Air Pollution
  • Water Pollution
  • Soil Pollution
  • Noise Pollution
  • Light Pollution
